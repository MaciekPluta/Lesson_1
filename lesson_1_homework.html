Simple Flax Seed Bread Recipe

Recipe based on Ken Forkish Book

Prep time

20 h

Ingrdients

  • 1000g flour
  • 750g water
  • 22g salt
  • 1/3 cup flax seed
  • 2g dried yeast

Preparation

  1. Mix 500g flour with 500g of the water
  2. Add 0.3 grams of yeast
  3. Mix and leave overnight to rise
  4. Add remaining water, flour, yeast and salt
  5. Mix and leave to rise for 3-4h
  6. Shape 2 loaves and leave them to prove for 1h
  7. Bake in a Dutch Oven for 30 min at 475F
  8. After 30 min take off the lid and bake for another 15 min

Nutritional Information

Not recomeded for anyone who is gluten-free

Recipe is base on Ken Forkish's Book Flour Water Salt Yeast: The Fundamentals of Artisan Bread and Pizza
